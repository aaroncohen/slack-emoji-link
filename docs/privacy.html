Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Slack Emoji Link (“the extension”) is developed by Aaron Cohen. This policy describes how the extension handles information when you use it.

Summary

  • The extension has no developer-operated servers.
  • Slack session data stays in your browser and is used only to call Slack on your behalf.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

What the extension accesses

To copy emoji between Slack workspaces you are logged into, the extension may access:

  • Slack session information from open Slack tabs (including workspace metadata stored in browser storage and session cookies needed for authenticated Slack API requests).
  • Custom emoji metadata and images from your source workspace, in order to upload or alias them in your chosen destination workspace.
  • Locally stored extension data (discovered workspace list and UI state) via the browser’s extension storage APIs.

What is stored locally

The extension stores discovered Slack workspace identifiers and related metadata in browser.storage.local on your device so you do not have to re-detect workspaces every session. This data is not transmitted to the developer.

What is transmitted

Network requests are made directly from your browser to Slack (*.slack.com, *.slack-edge.com) using your existing login session. The developer does not receive copies of those requests or responses.

The extension does not transmit data to analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party services operated by the developer.

Chrome Web Store Limited Use

If you install the extension from the Chrome Web Store, the extension’s use of information received from Slack APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements: data accessed via Slack is used only to provide the emoji copy functionality you request, is not transferred to others except as necessary to provide or improve user-facing features (direct communication with Slack using your session), and is not used for personalized advertising or sold to data brokers.

Permissions

The extension requests browser permissions solely to:

  • Detect Slack workspaces from tabs you already have open
  • Read and upload custom emoji via Slack’s APIs using your session
  • Persist discovered workspace metadata locally between sessions

Data retention and deletion

Local extension data remains until you remove the extension or clear extension storage in your browser. Uninstalling the extension removes locally stored extension data from your browser.

Children

The extension is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
